    • Hardware

    The Motherboard itself is a printed circuit board that allows the CPU, RAM, and all other computer hardware components to communicate with each other.
    The central processing unit (CPU) is the brain of your computer. It handles all the instructions you give your compute.

    RAM stands for Random Access Memory. RAM is like your conscience memory for your computer.

    Storage is the hard drive's responsibility. Everything you keep on your computer is on a hard drive.

    Computer components are connected to each other via thin connections known as 'bus' on the motherboard.

    a computer keyboard is a typewriter-style device, which uses an arrangement of buttons or keys, to act as mechanical levers or electronic switches.

    a mouse is a pointing device that detects two-dimensional motion relative to a surface.

    The LCD monitor, the most common kind of flat panel display. It is a newer technology than CRT. LCD monitors use much less desk space, are lightweight and use less electricity than CRT. They have been used for many years in the screens of laptop and notebook computers.
