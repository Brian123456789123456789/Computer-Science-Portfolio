    What is Vindinium.

    Vindinium is an artificial intelligence challenge. In this challenge you must
    program a bot to be able to survive in a randomly generated environment by
    telling him when to go claim free mines get health from taverns or attack other
    players

    How do the bots work

    The bots are already programmed to claim mines and move.With simple code
    you can have your bot travel to taverns to regain health and attack other players
    to claim their mines.When claiming other players mines by killing them your gold can go up faster
    many mines you have.
